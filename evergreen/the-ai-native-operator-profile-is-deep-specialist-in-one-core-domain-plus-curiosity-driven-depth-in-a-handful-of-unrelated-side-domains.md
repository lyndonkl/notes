# The AI-native operator profile is deep specialist in one core domain plus curiosity-driven depth in a handful of unrelated side domains

Source: [[thought-session-what-the-g-in-agi-actually-means|Thought Session: What the G in AGI Actually Means]]

The profile that captures AI's value most fully is neither the deep specialist (a thousand feet deep, narrow) nor the broad generalist (one foot deep, a thousand feet broad). It is a different shape: deep specialist in one core domain — the kind of depth that comes from a career — combined with meaningful, accumulated depth in a handful of unrelated side domains, picked up over years through curiosity-driven reading rather than professional necessity.

The mechanism is that AI now lets curiosity-driven knowledge be acted on. A reader who took two passes through a corporate finance and valuation course but never became a finance analyst could not previously act on that knowledge — implementing it required becoming a part-time specialist. Now the implementation cost has collapsed: the reader can encode what they learned as a skill, run it on real data, and empirically test whether the framework holds. The side-domain reading was always potential capability; AI converts it into actual capability.

This profile has been observed in my own practice — engineering (specifically infrastructure backend, with some machine learning) as the core specialty, with substantial side-domain reading in corporate finance, behavioral psychology, sports analytics, and several other areas — and the value of those side-domains for compositional AI work has been disproportionate to their share of my time. The autobiographical inflection should be acknowledged: the empirical evidence for this profile being valuable comes from my own practice, and broader generalizability is a hypothesis rather than a confirmed pattern.

**Open questions:**
- Is the profile causally productive (the deep + curiosity-broad shape itself produces AI-native value), or correlationally observed (the kind of person who acquires this shape is also the kind of person who builds systems well)? The two have different implications for whether others should pursue the profile deliberately.
- How many side domains is "a handful"? Is the value linear in the count, or does it have an optimum?
