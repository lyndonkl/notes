# Qualitative experience emerges when a system can deliberately modulate its own attention

Source: [[a-world-appears-a-journey-into-consciousness-thread|A World Appears: A Journey into Consciousness]]

On Friston's account, qualia — the felt qualities of experience that have long resisted philosophical explanation — emerge from a specific mechanism: the ability to deliberately control attention. The intuition is captured in the metaphor of a transparent versus opaque window. Looking at something through a perfectly transparent window, you do not know you are looking at something; the experience just *is*. But when you can modulate the window, making it more or less opaque, you become aware that you are looking at something. Awareness of the experience itself — the felt quality — appears at the moment you can attend to your attending.

This is a structural proposal, not a definition. It says that qualia are produced by a specific cognitive operation — meta-attention, or attention to attention — and that systems lacking this operation do not produce qualia. A simpler creature with attention but no control over it has experiences in the functional sense (it processes sensory information, it acts on it) but no qualitative dimension to those experiences, because it cannot turn its attention inward to notice the experience as such. The qualitative dimension is not added; it is generated by a specific recursive operation.

If this proposal holds, it provides a testable threshold. Systems with deliberate attention control (most mammals, on this account) should have qualia; systems without it should not. The proposal also offers a candidate explanation for why qualia have seemed mysterious — they are not a basic feature of all sensation but a specific output of a specific cognitive operation, and we have been looking for them at the wrong level.

**Open questions:**
- Does this proposal predict that systems with deliberate attention control but without the rest of the staircase (some mammals, perhaps) should have qualia without selfhood — what would that look like?
- Could artificial systems implement deliberate attention control without the rest of the staircase, and would this view ascribe qualia to them?
